About Me

Hi! My name is Benjamin (Benji) Moon and I am an aspiring user interface designer. I am currently a second year student at Drexel University studying UXID (User Experience Interaction Design). The thing I love the most about my major is that you have the ability and freedom to innovate. Innovation is such a key factor in UXID because of how generic everything is now.
